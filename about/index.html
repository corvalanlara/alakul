About us

Guest house Ala-Kol is located in the Altyn-Arashan gorge, 25 km east of the city of Karakol! The owners Uchkun, Zhazgul and Almaz have been welcoming guests from all over the world for more than 10 years, at an altitude of over 2800 m.

The guest house consists of 2 floors. Each floor has a large hall, two toilets and a shower. There is also a steam room-bathhouse, which works at the evenings. From the window of each cozy room there is a beautiful view of the snow-capped peaks of the Tien Shan mountains.

Every morning you will find a hearty breakfast: porridge made with fresh milk, sour cream and jam, fresh eggs, mountain honey and fresh bread. As evening falls, indulge in a delightful dinner crafted from wholesome, eco-friendly ingredients.
